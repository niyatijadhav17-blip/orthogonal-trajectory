Mathematical Results

Original Family: y = ax²
Orthogonal Trajectory Family: y² = -x²/(2a) + C
Differential Equation: dy/dx = 2ax

Calculation Steps

1
Start with the family of curves: F(x, y, a) = 0
2
Differentiate implicitly with respect to x to eliminate the parameter a
3
Obtain the differential equation: dy/dx = f(x, y)
4
For orthogonal trajectories, replace dy/dx with -dx/dy (or -1/(dy/dx))
5
Solve the new differential equation to find the orthogonal family
